Contents
--------
15 pixel-art characters, one folder each. Per character:

- <anim>.webp        the looping animation preview
- <anim>_sheet.png   the sprite sheet (square frames, left to right)

Animations vary per character (idle, walk, run, jump, attack, hurt,
death). Every sheet is a row of square frames; most are 8 frames, a
few are longer. Drop a sheet into your engine and slice it by its
frame width.

Made with the default SpriteCook pixel-art preset. Type any character
and get a matching one in the same style.
